Research & Publications

Mapping stress resilience from behavior to brain-wide activity.

I work with larval zebrafish to quantify stress resilience and relate it to whole-brain neural activity using pERK imaging, light-sheet calcium imaging, and graph-theoretic analyses.

Stress resilience assay in larval zebrafish

Manuscript in preparation

Standardized three-phase behavioral assay to classify resilient vs. vulnerable trajectories based on locomotor rebound after acute osmotic stress.

Brain-wide pERK mapping of resilience networks

Manuscript in preparation

Voxel- and region-level pERK/tERK analyses combined with graph theory to identify stress and resilience networks across the zebrafish brain.
